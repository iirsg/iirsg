Aims and Scope

Transaction of Investigation in Remote Sensing & GIS is a journal open access that aims to publish in all areas of the world and make them accessible online, free of restrictions or some other subscription for researchers, as the completeness and reliability of the findings and current developments in the style of original papers, review papers, case reports, short correspondence, etc.

CrossRef assigns DOIs to all written papers. Transaction of Investigation in Remote Sensing & GIS is going to keep up to date with the latest innovations in the field and articles from the Transaction of Investigation in Remote Sensing & GIS journal are going to reach readers directly via e-mail at no charge.

Open Access is a model for writing research papers, which can usually be published on the Web without restriction to the global community. Therefore, anyone with an internet connection can read all papers written under Open Access. Open Access project is actively sponsored by the Transaction of Investigation in Remote Sensing & GIS Review. All papers published by Transaction of Investigation in Remote Sensing & GIS journal are free to be accessible to everyone immediately after publication in abstracts and text in full (HTML, PDF and XML format).

As is the case today, Collective norms should remain the framework for implementing the correct allocation and fair use of the published work rather than copyright law.
